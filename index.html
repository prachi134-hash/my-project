Welcome to Our College Clubs

Discover your passion and connect with fellow students by joining one of our many exciting clubs. Whether you're into arts, technology, or sustainability — there's a place for you here! The clubs here at Cummins suits everyone’s enthusiasms and fits everyone on campus. Cummins is enhanced with many such clubs. These clubs help students cultivate leadership and team-building skills. These clubs are responsible for the never-ending stream of liveliness and events that happen in our college. Name technical, academic, environmental or cultural, everything and everyone is satisfied here.

About CCoEW

Cummins College of Engineering for Women (CCoEW), Nagpur, established in 2010 by MKSSS, is NAAC A+ accredited and affiliated to RTM Nagpur University. Empowering women engineers through academic excellence, research, and holistic development.
