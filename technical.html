Our Technical Societies

Discover the diverse range of technical societies that cater to various engineering disciplines and interests.

IEEE

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. Our student branch provides opportunities for professional development, technical learning, and networking with industry experts.

Electrical Engineering
Robotics & Automation
Communication Systems
AI & Machine Learning

ISTE

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is a national professional non-profit organization for the improvement of technical education. It provides quality training programs for teachers and students and facilitates career development through various technical activities and competitions.

Faculty Development
Technical Competitions
Curriculum Development
Student Mentorship

IETE

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is India's leading recognized professional society devoted to the advancement of electronics, telecommunication, and IT. It promotes research and development in these fields and provides a platform for knowledge sharing.

Telecommunications
Electronics Design
Networking
Mobile Technology

CSI

The Computer Society of India (CSI) is the first and largest body of computer professionals in India. It aims to facilitate research, knowledge sharing, and career enhancement for all categories of IT professionals while also inspiring and nurturing new entrants into the industry.

Software Development
Data Science
Cyber Security
Cloud Computing
