About the Club

The Coding Club focuses on developing coding skills and abilities of students, preparing them for future placements. It conducts workshops, coding competitions, peer-learning sessions, and daily practice sessions to enhance technical aptitude.

Activities Conducted
Problem Definition

Faculty provide problem definitions circulated to all year student groups. Evaluation is done next week.

Peer-to-Peer Learning

Students share skills learned from NPTEL, Coursera, etc., among friends and juniors to enhance learning.

Poster Making

Students prepare posters from journals and mini/minor projects. Best posters displayed on the notice board.

Front End Design

Students design attractive front ends using images, videos, links, pop-ups, etc. Evaluation based on creativity.
